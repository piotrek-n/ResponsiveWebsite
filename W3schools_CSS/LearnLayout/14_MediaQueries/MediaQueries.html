"Responsive Design" is the strategy of making a site that "responds" to the browser and device that it is being shown on... by looking awesome no matter what.

Media queries are the most powerful tool for doing this. Let's take our layout that uses percent widths and have it display in one column when the browser is too small to fit the menu in the sidebar:

  • Home
  • Taco Menu
  • Draft list
  • Hours
  • Directions
  • Contact
This example works just like the last one. Notice we put a clearfix on the container. It's not needed in this example, but it would be if the nav was longer than the non-floated content.
